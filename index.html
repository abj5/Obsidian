Sedimentary Rock (slow and boring)

Over millions of years, I get weathered and eroded by wind, rain, and ice. Little pieces of me weather off, get eroded into rivers, transported to the ocean, and buried under layers of sediment.

All that pressure compacts me into shale or sandstone. It's like going from a cool person with sunglasses to a boring cow who eats grass.

Timeline: Several million years.

Metamorphic Rock (The Makeover)

If I get buried deep underground, all that heat and pressure transforms me into metamorphic rock like quartzite or schist ‚Äì rocks that have been "cooked" but not melted.

Getting crushed and burned for millions of years isn't so bad.

Timeline: 1-2 million years.

Igneous Rock (The Reunion Tour)

THIS is the dream! If I get buried deep enough to melt again, I become magma! Then if that magma erupts, I become a NEW igneous rock.

Maybe I'll be obsidian again, or grow some crystals and become granite or basalt. It's like getting a do-over!

Timeline: Tens of millions of years.
